WHAT IS LEAGUE OF LEGENDS ?

League of legends is a game developed by RIOT GAMES

It was released in 2009 and has since become one of the most played and watched esports games in the world. The game features two teams of five players, each controlling a unique character called a champion, with the goal of destroying the opposing team's Nexus, the core structure located in their base.
